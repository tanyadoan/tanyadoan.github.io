Hi! I’m Tanya Doan.

As a Photo Editor specializing in storytelling and image research, I weave together compelling images to create stunning visual sets that highlight or promote stories, ideas, and products.

After growing up in California, I lived and worked in New York City, and am now based in Berlin as of late 2017. During my 12 years in NYC, I edited and produced photoshoots within the media/entertainment industry. I also did image research, licensing, and developed effective photo processing workflows.

I have collaborated with a wide variety of people including: creative directors, designers, marketing managers, retouchers, social media mavens, publicists, assignment editors, film & television producers, executives, software engineers, and of course, photographers.

I understand the balance between maintaining high quality standards while staying within budget and deadline, all while maintaining clear communication throughout the project.

I studied photography media in university, learned how to shoot using film on a vintage camera, and also taught myself digital imaging when it was still an emerging technology. 

My personal work can be viewed in this ongoing passion project.
